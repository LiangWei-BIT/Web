IVENTURE is the first falling-down VR game in the world.

Main Features: falling-down gameplay, infinite levels, fancy galaxy

Background: The story takes place in year 2235 when the human race has entered the era of aggressive space exploration. Christina, the main character of the game, has fallen on a mysterious planet in a failed space mission. The planet is covered by an endless series of debris. Christina must jump through the debris safely while she searches for her spaceship and finds her way out...
